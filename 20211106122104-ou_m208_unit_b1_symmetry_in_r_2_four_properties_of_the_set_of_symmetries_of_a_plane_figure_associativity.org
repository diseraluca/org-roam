\begin{tcolorbox}[title=Associativity property of symmetries]
Composition of symmetries is associative.

If $F$ is a plane figure, for all $f,g,h \in S(F)$,
\begin{equation*}
(f \circ g) \circ h = f \circ (g \circ h)
\end{equation*}
\end{tcolorbox}
