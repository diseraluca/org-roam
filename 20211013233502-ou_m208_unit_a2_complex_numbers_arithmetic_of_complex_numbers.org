- Arithmetic on complex number is carried out the same as for reals but we substitute \(i\) for \(-1\) every time it occurs.

#+LATEX_HEADER: \usepackage{tcolorbox}

\begin{tcolorbox}[title=Arithmetic of Complex Numbers]
Let $z_1 = x_1 + iy_1$ and $z_2 = x_2 + iy_2$ be any complex numbers.

Then the following operations can be applied:

\begin{itemize}
\item{Addition}
\begin{equation*}
z_1 + z_2 = (x_1 + x_2) + i(y_1 + y_2)
\end{equation*}
\item{Subtraction}
\begin{equation*}
z_1 - z_2 = (x_1 - x_2) + i(y_1 - y_2)
\end{equation*}
\item{Multiplication}
\begin{equation*}
z_1z_2 = (x_1x_2 - y_1y_2) + i(x_2y_1 - x_1y_2)
\end{equation*}
\end{itemize}
\end{tcolorbox}
