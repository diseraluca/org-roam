#+title: OU:M208:Unit-A3:Mathematical Statements:The Contrapositive of An Implication
#+filetags: OU M208 Mathematics Proofs Statements Implications Contrapositives

+ Contrapositive :: Given any implication "If P, then Q" its contrapostiive is "If not Q, then not P".

The contrapositive of an implication is equivalent to the original implication.
It is false when the implication is false and true when the implication is true.
