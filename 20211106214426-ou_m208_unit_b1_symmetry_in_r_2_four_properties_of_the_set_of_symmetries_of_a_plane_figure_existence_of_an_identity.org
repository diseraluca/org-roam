#+title: OU:M208:Unit-B1:Symmetry in R^2:Four Properties of the Set of Symmetries of a Plane Figure:Existence of an Identity
#+filetags: OU M208 Mathematics Group-Theory Symmetry Plane Identity

#+LATEX_HEADER: \usepackage{tcolorbox}

\begin{tcolorbox}
The set $S(F)$ of symmetries of a plane figure $F$ contains a special symmetry $e$

(the \textbf{identity symmetry}) such that, for each symmetry $f$ in $S(F)$:

\begin{equation*}
f \circ e = f = e \circ f
\end{equation*}
\end{tcolorbox}
